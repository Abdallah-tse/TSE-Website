About TSE

4 questions

Founded in 2003 and headquartered in Cairo, Egypt, Travel Solutions Egypt (TSE) is a leading technology company specialising in software solutions for the travel industry. With over two decades of combined experience in travel operations and software development, TSE delivers reliable, efficient, and scalable systems for tour operators and ground handlers.

TSE primarily serves incoming tour operators and global ground handlers who need mission-critical automation systems. Our clients include companies such as Wings, Escapade, Gezira, YouGo, CTT, Traveline, Bright Star, and Blue Sky — all leading names in the Egyptian inbound travel market.

TSE offers three core products:

  • ITOMS — a comprehensive incoming tour operator management system covering reservations, accounting, allotments, and reporting.
  • Archiving System — a document filing and retrieval platform fully integrated with ITOMS.
  • B2B Portal — a web-based booking and reservation platform for travel and tourism services across Egypt.

You can reach us by email at tse@tsegypt.com, by phone or WhatsApp at +201555729580, or visit our contact page to send us a message directly. Our team is based in Cairo, Egypt.

ITOMS

6 questions

ITOMS (Incoming Tour Operators Management System) is a comprehensive sales support, reservation, and management system built on state-of-the-art computer technology and advanced software design concepts. It covers every aspect of the tour-operating business — from costing and pricing to operational accounting and management reporting.

ITOMS ships with a comprehensive database covering hotels and Nile Cruises in Egypt, transport schedules, and information about the majority of guides in Egypt. The system is ready to use almost immediately once contracted rates are fed in — no extensive data-entry setup is required.

Yes. ITOMS is an in-house, multi-user system. It does not require on-site programming staff for its daily operation and maintenance. Multiple team members can work simultaneously across reservations, traffic, accounts, and management reporting modules.

Yes. ITOMS is a fully multi-language and multi-currency system. It manages foreign currency differentials seamlessly and produces invoices and reports in the required currency, making it ideal for operators handling international clients.

ITOMS covers the full spectrum of inbound tour operations, including:

  • Booking & reservation management
  • Allotment control (hotels, guides, transport)
  • Traffic department management
  • Accounts payable and receivable
  • Planning, costing, and pricing
  • Management and research reports
  • Client manifest and itinerary generation

Yes. ITOMS is developed in standard travel industry format, which enables the exchange of information with other reservation systems. It also supports direct Web API integrations for supplier connectivity and real-time data sync.

Archiving System

4 questions

The TSE Archiving System (also called Travel Filing System) is a digital document management solution fully integrated with ITOMS. It eliminates physical filing by letting staff drag-and-drop emails, scan faxes, and attach documents directly to agencies, suppliers, and booking records (PNRs).

The system handles a wide range of travel documents, including:

  • Vouchers, service orders, and payment orders
  • Itineraries and request forms
  • Invoices, debit notes, and credit notes
  • Incoming and outgoing emails
  • Scanned faxes and physical documents
  • Rate confirmations and supplier correspondence

There are two retrieval methods. First, directly from within ITOMS by opening the relevant PNR, agency, or supplier screen and clicking the attachment tab. Second, via a web-based search interface accessible from any browser — allowing staff to view, download, or print saved documents remotely.

Yes. In addition to the built-in categories (incoming, outgoing, rates, confirmation), you can create your own custom categories to organise documents in a way that best suits your company's workflow — making search and retrieval faster and more intuitive.

B2B Portal

4 questions

The B2B Portal is a web-based application designed to simplify and automate the booking and reservation process for travel and tourism services across Egypt. It gives customers a single, user-friendly interface to search, book, and manage travel packages, hotels, transport, and tourism activities.

The portal is designed for travel trade partners — including agencies, operators, and resellers — who need to access and book Egypt-based travel products on behalf of their clients. It streamlines the B2B booking workflow and reduces manual communication between parties.

Through the B2B Portal, users can book and manage:

  • Travel packages and tour programs
  • Hotel accommodations
  • Transportation services
  • Tourism activities and excursions

Yes. The B2B Portal connects directly to ITOMS, meaning bookings made through the portal flow into the same back-office system used by your operations team — ensuring live availability, consistent pricing, and automatic record creation without double-entry.

Technical & Support

5 questions

ITOMS is built on the .NET Framework with Windows Forms for the desktop interface, MySQL as the database engine, and IIS Server for web components. The system is locally hosted and secured with SSL/TLS encryption. Reporting is handled through Crystal Reports and supplemented by Web APIs for external integrations.

ITOMS is currently deployed as a locally hosted system, giving clients full control over their data on their own infrastructure. TSE also offers cloud-ready containerised deployments with elastic scaling designed to match peak operational seasons — please contact us to discuss the best deployment model for your organisation.

TSE targets a 99.9% operational environment uptime across its deployed systems. Our architecture is designed for high availability, with optimised algorithms ensuring rapid database response times even under maximum workloads.

No on-site programming staff are required for ITOMS's day-to-day operation and maintenance. The system is designed to be fully managed by travel operations staff without technical expertise. TSE provides ongoing support and updates as part of our service agreement.
