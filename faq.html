General

Our systems are purpose-built for incoming tour operators and ground handlers operating in Egypt. They are particularly suited to companies managing high-volume reservations, multi-currency transactions, multi-language operations, and complex allotment management.

ITOMS

ITOMS stands for Incoming Tour Operators Management System. It is a comprehensive sales support, reservation, and management platform covering every aspect of tour operating — including costing, pricing, planning, allotment control, traffic department management, accounts payable and receivable, and detailed management reports.

Yes. ITOMS is a fully multi-language and multi-currency system. This enables tour operators serving international clients to process reservations and financial records in the currencies and languages relevant to each market.

Yes. ITOMS is developed in standard travel industry format, which enables the exchange of information with other reservation systems. This interoperability makes it practical to integrate with partner systems and global distribution channels.

ITOMS includes research reports and data analysis tools designed to help tour operators understand past, present, and future dynamics of their business. Reports cover management overviews, allotment status, traffic department operations, and full financial summaries.

Archiving

The Travel Filing System (Archiving module) is designed to reduce operational costs by eliminating physical filing. It saves time, minimizes paper consumption, and frees up physical office space. Documents can be viewed, downloaded, and printed from anywhere via the internet.

The archiving module is fully integrated with ITOMS. Vouchers, service orders, payment orders, itineraries, invoices, debit and credit notes are automatically attached to their corresponding PNR. You can also drag incoming emails directly into a PNR, or scan incoming faxes and documents straight from the PNR screen.

There are two ways to access saved documents: directly from the PNR or agency/supplier screen by clicking the Attachments tab inside ITOMS, or by using the web-based search screen accessible from any internet connection. You can also create custom categories beyond the default ones (incoming, outgoing, rates, confirmation) to simplify retrieval.

B2B

The B2B System is a web-based application that simplifies and automates booking and reservation management for travel and tourism services across Egypt. It allows customers to search, book, and manage travel packages, hotel accommodations, transportation, and tourism activities — all through a single, user-friendly interface.

Yes. The B2B platform is entirely web-based, meaning your partners and clients can access it from any device with an internet connection — without needing to install any software.

Yes. The B2B platform is designed to handle multiple service types in a single booking flow — including hotel accommodations, transportation, packaged tours, and tourism activities — reducing the friction of using separate systems for each service category.
